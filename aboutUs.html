About Us



Our Goal



A Commitment to Innovation Redefining Outdoor Living Solutions

With our advanced manufacturing capabilities and highly skilled team, we are dedicated to reimagining outdoor spaces. Our unique solutions include:


  • Folding glass doors and windows
  • Stackable glass doors and windows
  • Vertical sliding glass doors and windows
  • Movable aluminum solar roofs
  • Movable PVC solar roofs
  • Retractable insect screen
  • Vertical sliding insect screen

Why Us



Energy conservation

Our glass systems are highly efficient in energy conservation, as they use double insulating glass along with profiles designed with multiple channels and a double seal gasket,
which reduces heat transfer and results in energy savings.


Weather protection

Our special additives are impact modifiers to improve strength, water tightness, durability, and low maintenance.


Sound insulation

The aluminum frames are airtight, leaving no gaps between the frame and the glass, preventing the entry of dust and noise, and maintaining the user's comfort.
