| Folding door and window system

Folding door and window systems are characterized by seamlessly connecting indoor and outdoor spaces. These systems feature high insulation due to the gasket applied on all four sides of each panel. When fully opened, the space becomes free of aluminum partitions. They are ideal for hotels, cafes, outdoor lounges, office partitions, and interior partitions in homes. The double-glazed glass is resistant to water, heat, and dust, and reduces sound and noise.

| Glass partitions

Glass partitions or dividers are used to separate spaces within a larger area. Glass, being the primary material for these partitions, allows natural light to pass through, maintaining an open feel while providing separation between areas. Additionally, glass partitions enhance the aesthetic appeal of a space. Commonly used in offices, homes, and other spaces, these partitions create distinct zones without sacrificing natural light or visual connection. Commonly used.
| Motorized panoramic glass system

The Panorama Glass System (hinged) is a movable railing that is remotely controlled and equipped with a motor and chain. The glass is double-glazed, making it resistant to rain, heat, and dust, and it can be cleaned from both the inside and outside. When the glass is opened downwards, it aligns to form a railing. It is easy to use and ideal for use in hotels, cafes, and outdoor lounges.
